<p><p>Electronic grade gases are high purity gases used in various processes in semiconductor manufacturing. Deposition gases are used to deposit thin films on surfaces. Etching gases are used to selectively remove material from a substrate. Doping gases are used to introduce impurities into the semiconductor to alter its electrical properties. Lithography gases are used in the process of creating patterns on semiconductor wafers. Each type of gas plays a crucial role in the fabrication of electronic devices with high precision and reliability.</p></p>
