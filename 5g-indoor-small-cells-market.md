<p><p>5G Indoor Small Cells are divided into two main market types: Sub-6GHz and mmWAVE. Sub-6GHz small cells operate at frequencies below 6GHz, providing wider coverage and better penetration through walls. They are suitable for indoor deployments in various settings such as offices, shopping malls, and residential buildings. On the other hand, mmWAVE small cells operate at higher frequencies, offering higher data rates but limited coverage. They are commonly used in densely populated areas such as stadiums, airports, and concert venues for high-capacity indoor deployments.</p></p>
