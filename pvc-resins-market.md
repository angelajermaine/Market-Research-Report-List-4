<p><p>PVC Resins market can be classified into two main types based on production processes: Ethylene Process and Calcium Carbide Process. Ethylene Process involves the polymerization of vinyl chloride using ethylene as a raw material, whereas Calcium Carbide Process uses calcium carbide to produce vinyl chloride monomer. Both processes have their own advantages and limitations in terms of cost, efficiency, and environmental impact. The choice between the two processes depends on various factors such as availability of raw materials and market demand.</p></p>
