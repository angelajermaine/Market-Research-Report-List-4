<p><strong>The Laboratory Tube Furnace Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Zone Furnace</li><li>Multi Zone Furnace</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Laboratory tube furnaces are available in two main types - single zone furnace and multi zone furnace. Single zone furnaces have a uniform temperature throughout the heating process, suitable for simpler applications. On the other hand, multi zone furnaces have different temperature zones along the heating tube, allowing for more complex heating profiles and better control over the heating process. The choice between the two types depends on the specific requirements of the experiment or process being carried out in the laboratory.</p></p>
