<p><p>Amplifiers are electronic devices that increase the amplitude of a signal. The market for amplifiers can be categorized into several types based on their operating class. Class-A amplifiers provide high-quality sound but are less efficient. Class-B amplifiers are more efficient but may introduce distortion. Class-A/B amplifiers combine the qualities of Class-A and Class-B. Class-G and H amplifiers improve efficiency by using multiple power supplies. Class-D amplifiers are highly efficient as they rapidly switch on and off to amplify the signal.</p></p>
