<p><p>Electromagnetic diaphragm metering pumps are available in two main market types: manual control and automatic control. Manual control pumps require the operator to adjust the flow rate and stroke length manually, making them ideal for applications where precise control is not critical. Automatic control pumps, on the other hand, have built-in mechanisms for adjusting the flow rate and stroke length automatically, offering more precise and consistent dosing for applications where accuracy is key. Both types of pumps have their own set of advantages and are chosen based on the specific needs of the application.</p></p>
