<p><strong>The Ball Screw Support Bearing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Row</li><li>Double Row</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ball screw support bearings are classified into two main types: single row and double row. Single row bearings have a single row of balls that run between the inner and outer rings, providing support and stability for the ball screw. Double row bearings, on the other hand, have two rows of balls that run parallel to each other, offering increased load carrying capacity and rigidity. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring the smooth and efficient operation of ball screws in various industrial applications.</p></p>
