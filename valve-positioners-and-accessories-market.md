Pneumatic positioners are simpler and cost-effective, utilizing compressed air to control valve positioning. Electro-pneumatic positioners, on the other hand, offer more precise control by combining electrical signals with pneumatic forces. Accessories in this market can include mounting kits, feedback sensors, and communication modules. These products are essential for ensuring accurate and efficient operation of valves in various industrial applications.</p></p>
