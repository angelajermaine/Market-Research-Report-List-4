<p><p>Thermoplastic Polyurethanes (TPU) are versatile materials used in various industries due to their unique properties such as flexibility, durability, and resistance to chemicals. There are three main types of TPU in the market: Polyester-based TPU, Polyether-based TPU, and Polycaprolactone-based TPU. Polyester-based TPU offers high mechanical strength and abrasion resistance, while Polyether-based TPU provides excellent flexibility and low-temperature performance. Polycaprolactone-based TPU offers biodegradability and is commonly used in environmentally conscious applications. Each type caters to specific needs and applications in different industries.</p></p>
