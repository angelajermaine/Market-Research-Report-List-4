<p><p>A manual tile cutter is a hand-operated tool used for cutting ceramic, porcelain, or glass tiles to a desired size or shape. It consists of a sharp scoring wheel that is drawn across the surface of the tile to create a score line, which is then snapped using a lever to break the tile along the line. Manual tile cutters are commonly used by DIY enthusiasts, contractors, and tile installers for various tiling projects.</p><p>The Manual Tile Cutter Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 3.20% during the forecast period.
