<p><strong>The Touchscreen Display Module Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Capacitive Touchscreen Display Module</li><li>Resistive Touchscreen Display Module</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Touchscreen Display Modules are classified based on the type of technology used to detect touch input. Capacitive Touchscreen Display Modules use a grid of sensors to detect the electrical properties of the human body and are more durable and responsive than Resistive Touchscreen Display Modules, which rely on pressure to detect touch input. Capacitive displays are popular for their multitouch capabilities, while Resistive displays are more cost-effective and resistive to scratches. Both types of modules are widely used in devices such as smartphones, tablets, and industrial equipment.</p></p>
