<p><p>UV Photoinitiators are chemicals used in various industries such as printing, coatings, and adhesives to initiate the curing process when exposed to UV light. There are two main types of UV photoinitiators: free-radical type and cationic type. Free-radical type photoinitiators initiate the process by generating free radicals upon exposure to UV light, while cationic type photoinitiators work by forming cations. Both types play a crucial role in the efficiency and effectiveness of UV curing processes in different applications.</p></p>
