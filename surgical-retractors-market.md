<p><p>Surgical retractors are devices used during surgical procedures to hold back tissues and organs to provide a clear view for the surgeon. There are two main types of surgical retractors - Hand Held Retractors, which are manually operated by a surgical assistant, and Self-Retaining Retractors, which can hold themselves in place without the need for constant manual adjustment. Hand Held Retractors are more versatile and widely used, while Self-Retaining Retractors are preferred for longer surgeries or when a consistent level of retraction is required.</p></p>
