<p><p>Expandable hoses are a type of marine hose that can expand in length when filled with water and contract when empty. This makes them ideal for storage and transportation. Porous or perforated hoses have tiny holes along their length, allowing water to seep out slowly for watering plants or controlling erosion. Both types of hoses are popular in the marine industry for their versatility and convenience in various applications.</p></p>
