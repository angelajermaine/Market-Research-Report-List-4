<p><p>B-type ultrasound diagnostic systems are categorized into three main types based on their scanning capabilities. Fanshaped scanning systems emit ultrasound waves in a wide fan-shaped pattern for a broad view of internal structures. Linear scanning systems emit waves in a straight line for detailed images of specific areas. Mixed type systems combine both fanshaped and linear scanning techniques for a comprehensive examination. Each market type offers distinct advantages in terms of imaging depth, resolution, and versatility in diagnosing various medical conditions.</p></p>
