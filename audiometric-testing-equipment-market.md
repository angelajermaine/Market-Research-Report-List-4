<p><p>Audiometric testing equipment market can be divided into two main types: Screening Audiometry and Diagnostic Audiometry. Screening Audiometry involves quick and simple tests to determine if further testing is needed. This type of testing is often used in schools or workplaces to identify potential hearing issues. On the other hand, Diagnostic Audiometry is more comprehensive and specific, aiming to diagnose the exact nature and extent of hearing loss or other auditory issues. These tests are typically carried out by trained audiologists in clinical settings.</p></p>
