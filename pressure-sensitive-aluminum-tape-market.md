<p><p>Pressure sensitive aluminum tape is available in two main types: Single coated tape and double coated tape. Single coated tape has adhesive applied to only one side of the aluminum foil, making it suitable for applications where one surface needs to be adhered to a substrate. On the other hand, double coated tape has adhesive applied to both sides of the foil, allowing for bonding between two surfaces simultaneously. Both types offer strong adhesion and are commonly used in HVAC, construction, and industrial applications.</p></p>
