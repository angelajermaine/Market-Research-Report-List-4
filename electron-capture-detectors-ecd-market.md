<p><p>Electron Capture Detectors (ECD) are used in gas chromatography to detect electron capturing compounds. There are two main types of ECDs in the market: Radioactive Electron Capture Detectors use a radioactive source to produce electrons for capturing, while Non-radioactive Electron Capture Detectors use a non-radioactive source such as a tritium generator or nickel-63 foil. Both types provide high sensitivity and selectivity for detecting compounds with electron capturing properties, making them valuable tools in analytical chemistry applications.</p></p>
