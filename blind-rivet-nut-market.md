<p><p>A blind rivet nut, also known as a rivet nut or nutsert, is a fastener that is used to provide a threaded hole in materials where only one side is accessible for installation. These nuts are commonly used in applications where it is difficult to create a tapped hole, such as in thin or brittle materials. Blind rivet nuts are typically made of materials such as steel, aluminum, or stainless steel, and come in a variety of sizes and thread types to accommodate different requirements.</p><p>The Blind Rivet Nut Market is experiencing significant growth, with a projected CAGR of 9.6% during the forecast period.
