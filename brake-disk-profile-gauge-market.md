<p><p>The Brake Disk Profile Gauge market includes two main types - Wheel Mounted and Axle Mounted. Wheel Mounted gauges are directly attached to the wheel hub, allowing for easy measurement of brake disk profiles while the wheel is in motion. Axle Mounted gauges, on the other hand, are mounted on the axle of the vehicle, providing a stationary reference point for accurate measurements. Both types offer convenience and accuracy in assessing brake disk profiles, catering to different preferences and needs in the market.</p></p>
