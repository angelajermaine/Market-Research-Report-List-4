<p><p>The Universal Joint market consists of two main types: Constant Velocity Joints and Cross-axis Universal Joints. Constant Velocity Joints are designed to maintain a constant velocity of rotation regardless of the angle of the joint, making them ideal for high-performance vehicles like sports cars. Cross-axis Universal Joints, on the other hand, are used to connect shafts at offset angles, allowing for smooth transmission of power in various applications such as industrial machinery and automotive drivetrains. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring efficient power transmission and smooth operation in a wide range of industries.</p></p>
