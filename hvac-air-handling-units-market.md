<p><strong>The HVAC Air Handling Units Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Draw Through Type</li><li>Blow Through Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>AHU market consists of two main types - Draw Through Type and Blow Through Type. In Draw Through Type, the fan is positioned at the end of the unit where air is drawn through the coil before being discharged into the space. On the other hand, in Blow Through Type, the fan is located before the coil, where air is blown through the coil to cool or heat it before being discharged. Both types play a crucial role in HVAC systems by controlling airflow and temperature within a building.</p></p>
