<p><p>Horizontal Lifeline System market types include Gallows System, Lanyard System, Strap System, and Others. The Gallows System consists of vertical posts with horizontal beams suspended between them. The Lanyard System utilizes a flexible line attached to a worker's harness for mobility along the horizontal lifeline. The Strap System incorporates straps instead of ropes for enhanced durability and strength. Other horizontal lifeline systems may include variations or combinations of these types for specific safety needs in various industries.</p></p>
