<p><p>In-line 3D Solder Paste Inspection Equipment is divided into two main types: Laser-based and PMP-based systems. Laser-based equipment uses lasers to create a 3D representation of the solder paste, allowing for high precision inspection. PMP-based systems, on the other hand, utilize phase measuring profilometry technology to accurately measure the height and volume of the solder paste. Both types of equipment are essential for ensuring the quality and reliability of electronic assemblies during the manufacturing process.</p></p>
