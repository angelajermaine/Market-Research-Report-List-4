<p><p>Well-control fluids are essential in oil and gas drilling operations to maintain pressure and prevent blowouts. Brine-based fluids, such as sodium chloride solutions, are commonly used for their high density and compatibility with formation water. Polymer-based fluids, on the other hand, consist of synthetic polymers that provide viscosity and flow control. These fluids are especially effective in high temperature and high-pressure environments. Both brine and polymer well-control fluids play a crucial role in ensuring safe and efficient drilling operations in the oil and gas industry.</p></p>
