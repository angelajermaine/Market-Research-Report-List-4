<p><p>The Air Container Market is divided into two main types - Lower Deck Containers and Main Deck Containers. Lower Deck Containers are placed in the lower compartment of the aircraft and are typically used for bulk cargo or luggage. Main Deck Containers, on the other hand, are located on the main deck of the aircraft and are used for larger or more valuable cargo. Each type of container serves a specific purpose and plays a crucial role in the efficient transportation of goods via air.</p></p>
