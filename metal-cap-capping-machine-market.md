<p><p>Metal cap capping machines are available in two main market types: semi-automatic and automatic. Automatic machines, on the other hand, are fully automated and can perform the capping process without any human assistance. Semi-automatic machines are often preferred for smaller-scale operations or when flexibility is needed, while automatic machines are more commonly used in higher volume production environments for efficiency and consistency.</p></p>
