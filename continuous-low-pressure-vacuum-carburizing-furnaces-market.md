<p><p>Continuous low pressure vacuum carburizing furnaces are available in three main types: Single Chamber, Double Chamber, and Multi Chamber. Single Chamber furnaces are designed for small-scale production, while Double Chamber furnaces allow for increased throughput by having two separate chambers for carburizing and quenching. Multi Chamber furnaces are the most versatile option, with multiple chambers allowing for simultaneous carburizing and quenching of different parts. Each type offers unique advantages and is suitable for different production requirements in the heat treatment industry.</p></p>
