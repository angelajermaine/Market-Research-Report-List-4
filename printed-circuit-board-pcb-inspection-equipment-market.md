<p><p>Printed Circuit Board (PCB) Inspection Equipment market includes Automatic Optical Inspection (AOI) and Automatic X-Ray Inspection (AXI). AOI uses optical systems to scan PCBs for defects such as missing components or misalignments. AXI utilizes X-ray technology to inspect hidden solder joints and identify defects that may not be visible to the naked eye. Both technologies are essential for ensuring the quality and reliability of PCBs in various industries such as electronics manufacturing.</p></p>
