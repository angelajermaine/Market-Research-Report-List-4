<p><p>Polymer-Dispersed Liquid Crystals (PDLCs) are a type of smart materials that combine the properties of both polymers and liquid crystals. There are different types of PDLCs in the market, including Scattering PDLCs, Nano-PDLCs, and Polymer Network Liquid Crystals. Scattering PDLCs disperse liquid crystals in a polymer matrix, Nano-PDLCs contain nano-sized particles for improved optical properties, and Polymer Network Liquid Crystals have a polymer network structure that controls liquid crystal alignment. These diverse PDLCs offer a range of applications in display technology and optical devices.</p></p>
