<p><strong>The Sapphire Lenses Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Concave Lens</li><li>Convex Lens</li><li>Planoconvex Lens</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Sapphire lenses are available in various types including concave lens, convex lens, and planoconvex lens. Concave lenses are thinner in the center and thicker at the edges, causing light rays to diverge. Convex lenses are thicker in the center and thinner at the edges, causing light rays to converge. Planoconvex lenses have one flat and one curved surface, focusing light rays to a single point. These different types of lenses serve different purposes and are used in various industries such as optics, electronics, and medical devices.</p></p>
