<p><p>Pulmonary Arterial Hypertension Treatment Market comprises different types of medications such as Vasodilators, Phosphodiesterase 5 (PDE 5) Inhibitors, Endothelin Receptor Antagonists (ERA), and Soluble Guanylate Cyclase (SGC) Stimulators. Vasodilators help widen blood vessels, reducing pressure in the arteries. PDE 5 inhibitors prevent the breakdown of a substance that promotes relaxation of the arteries. ERAs block the effects of endothelin, a hormone that can constrict blood vessels. SGC stimulators increase levels of another substance that promotes relaxation of blood vessels.</p></p>
