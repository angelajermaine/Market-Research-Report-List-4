<p><p>Multi-turn Absolute Rotary Encoders are devices used to measure the angular position of a rotating shaft. They provide absolute position information by counting the number of complete revolutions (multi-turns) as well as the current angular position (single-turn). This makes them particularly useful in applications where knowing the exact position is critical, such as in aerospace, robotics, and industrial automation.</p><p>The Multi-turn Absolute Rotary Encoders Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 6.6% during the forecast period.
