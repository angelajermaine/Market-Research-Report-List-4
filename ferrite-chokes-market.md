<p><p>Ferrite Chokes are used to suppress high-frequency electromagnetic interference in electronic circuits. Chip Ferrite Chokes are small and compact, making them ideal for PCB applications where space is limited. Through Hole Ferrite Chokes, on the other hand, are larger and are mounted directly onto the circuit board. They are commonly used in power supply applications where higher current ratings are required. Both types of Ferrite Chokes help to improve the overall performance and reliability of electronic devices by reducing unwanted noise and interference.</p></p>
