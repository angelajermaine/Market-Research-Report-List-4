<p><p>Digital optical micrometers are available in two main market types: single-axis and multi-axis. Single-axis micrometers allow for measurements along a single axis, providing precision in one direction. On the other hand, multi-axis micrometers enable measurements along multiple axes simultaneously, offering increased versatility and efficiency in measuring complex geometries. Both types of micrometers are widely used in industries such as manufacturing, automotive, and aerospace for precise measurement and quality control purposes.</p></p>
