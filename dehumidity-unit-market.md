<p><p>Dehumidity units come in various types to suit different needs. Cooling dehumidifiers use refrigeration technology to remove moisture from the air. Rotary dehumidifiers rotate a wheel coated with a desiccant material to absorb moisture. Pipeline dehumidifiers are installed within HVAC systems to dehumidify the air. Electroosmosis dehumidifiers use an electrical field to remove moisture from the air. Each type of dehumidity unit offers unique benefits and is suitable for specific applications.</p></p>
