<p><p>Medium Density Fibre Boards (MDF) are categorized into Type I and Type II markets based on their characteristics. Type I MDF boards are denser, stronger, and more durable, making them suitable for high-end furniture and construction applications. On the other hand, Type II MDF boards are less dense and more affordable, making them ideal for general construction and DIY projects. Both types offer a smooth surface for easy painting and finishing, but their differences lie in their strength and durability levels.</p></p>
