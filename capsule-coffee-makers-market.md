<p><ul><li>Closed Source System</li><li>Open Source System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Closed Source System capsule coffee makers are designed to only be compatible with specific brand capsules, limiting consumer choices. These systems typically have a higher initial cost for the machine but offer a wide variety of capsule flavor options. On the other hand, Open Source System capsule coffee makers allow for greater flexibility as they are compatible with various brands of capsules. While these machines may have a lower upfront cost, they may have fewer capsule options available compared to Closed Source Systems.</p></p>
