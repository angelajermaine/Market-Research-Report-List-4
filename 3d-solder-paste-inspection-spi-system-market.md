<p><p>3D Solder Paste Inspection (SPI) System market includes off-line SPI System and in-line SPI System. Off-line SPI Systems are used for post-production inspection where the PCB is removed from the production line for testing. In-line SPI Systems are integrated into the production line for real-time inspection of solder paste before components are placed. Both types of systems play a critical role in ensuring the quality and reliability of electronic assemblies by identifying defects in solder paste application.</p></p>
