<p><p>Needle-free connectors in the medical industry come in three types: Positive Pressure, Negative Pressure, and Neutral Pressure. Positive Pressure connectors have a higher pressure within the connector than in the bloodstream, facilitating rapid medication administration. Negative Pressure connectors have a lower pressure within the connector, preventing blood reflux and reducing the risk of infection. Neutral Pressure connectors work by preventing the continuous flow of fluids, providing a balance between positive and negative pressure. Each type of connector has its own advantages and applications in various medical settings.</p></p>
