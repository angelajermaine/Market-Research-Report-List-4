<p><p>Thermoforming techniques market includes various types such as Pressure Forming, Vacuum Forming, Matched Mold Forming, Twin Sheet Forming, and Mechanical Forming. Pressure Forming applies pressure to the plastic sheet during heating, Vacuum Forming uses vacuum to form the sheet over a mold, Matched Mold Forming uses two molds to create intricate shapes, Twin Sheet Forming fuses two sheets together, and Mechanical Forming uses mechanical force to shape the plastic. These techniques cater to different needs and applications in the manufacturing industry.</p></p>
