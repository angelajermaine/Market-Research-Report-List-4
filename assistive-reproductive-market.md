Artificial Insemination-Intrauterine Insemination (AI-IUI) involves placing sperm directly into the uterus to increase the chance of fertilization. Frozen Embryo Replacement (FER) involves thawing and transferring previously frozen embryos into the uterus. Other methods in this market may include surrogacy and gamete donation.</p></p>
