<p><strong>The Oxygen Barrier Pipes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PE-RT Oxygen Barrier Pipes</li><li>PEX Oxygen Barrier Pipes</li><li>PB Oxygen Barrier Pipes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Oxygen barrier pipes are categorized into three main types: PE-RT, PEX, and PB. PE-RT oxygen barrier pipes are made of a type of polyethylene that resists high temperatures and pressure, making them suitable for hot water applications. PEX oxygen barrier pipes are cross-linked polyethylene pipes that offer flexibility and durability, ideal for various heating and plumbing systems. PB oxygen barrier pipes are made of polybutylene material, providing excellent resistance to corrosion and chemical damage. Each type of oxygen barrier pipe offers specific advantages for different applications.</p></p>
