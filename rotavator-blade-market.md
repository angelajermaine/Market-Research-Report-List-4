<p><p>Rotavator blades are available in three main types: L blades, C blades, and J blades. L blades are designed with a sharp edge and are ideal for cutting through tough and compacted soil. C blades have a curved design which allows for better mixing and leveling of the soil. J blades are shaped like the letter J and are best suited for shallow cultivation and mixing of soil. Each type of blade is tailored to specific soil conditions and agricultural tasks to ensure optimal performance.</p></p>
