<p><p>The Automatic Wrap Around Case Packer market can be categorized into two main types: Continuous and Intermittent. Continuous case packers are designed to handle high-speed production lines where products are continuously fed into the machine without stopping. On the other hand, Intermittent case packers operate with pauses between each packaging cycle, making them suitable for lower-speed production lines or when multiple packaging formats are required. Both types serve different production needs and offer unique advantages depending on the specific requirements of the manufacturing process.</p></p>
