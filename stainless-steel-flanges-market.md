<p><p>Stainless steel flanges market includes various types such as Weld Flange, Blind Flange, Slip-On Flange, and Others. Weld Flanges are designed for connecting pipes by welding, whereas Blind Flanges are used to block off a pipeline. Slip-On Flanges slide over the pipe and are then welded in place. Other types of flanges include Threaded Flanges, Socket Weld Flanges, and Lap Joint Flanges, each serving specific purposes in connecting pipes and valves in various industrial applications.</p></p>
