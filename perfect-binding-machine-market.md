<p><p>Perfect binding machine market can be classified into two main types: automatic and semi-automatic. Automatic binding machines require minimal manual intervention, as they can perform most of the binding process automatically. These machines are efficient for high-volume production. Semi-automatic binding machines, on the other hand, require some manual operation in feeding pages, aligning edges, and pressing the book block. They are suitable for smaller scale production or for those who prefer more control over the binding process.</p></p>
