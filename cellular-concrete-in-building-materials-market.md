<p><p>Cellular concrete, also known as foam concrete or aerated concrete, is a lightweight building material that is produced by mixing a cementitious material with water and a foaming agent to create air bubbles within the mixture. This results in a material that has a low density, high strength, good acoustic insulation, and thermal insulation properties. Cellular concrete is commonly used for diverse applications in construction such as wall panels, roof insulation, and geotechnical fills.</p><p>The Cellular Concrete in Building Materials Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 7.7% during the forecast period.
