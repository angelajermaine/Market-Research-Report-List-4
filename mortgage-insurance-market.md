<p><strong>The Mortgage Insurance Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Borrower-Paid Mortgage Insurance</li><li>Lender-Paid Mortgage Insurance</li><li>FHA Mortgage Insurance</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Mortgage insurance is typically required for borrowers who put down less than 20% on their home purchase. Borrower-Paid Mortgage Insurance is when the borrower pays an additional monthly premium. Lender-Paid Mortgage Insurance is when the lender covers the cost of insurance by charging a higher interest rate. FHA Mortgage Insurance is specific to loans insured by the Federal Housing Administration and is mandatory for borrowers making a down payment of less than 10%. Each type of insurance serves to protect the lender in case the borrower defaults on the loan.</p></p>
