<p><p>Air pollution control systems market includes various types of technologies such as scrubbers, catalytic converters, and electrostatic precipitators. Scrubbers are used to remove pollutants from gas streams through a liquid spray. Catalytic converters convert harmful gases into less harmful substances through chemical reactions. Electrostatic precipitators use an electrostatic charge to remove particles from gas streams. These technologies play a crucial role in reducing air pollution and improving air quality by effectively removing harmful pollutants from industrial emissions and vehicle exhaust.</p></p>
