<p><p>Electromechanical testing machines use electric motors to apply force and perform various mechanical tests on materials. They are widely used in industries such as aerospace, automotive, and manufacturing. Hydraulic testing machines, on the other hand, use hydraulic fluid to generate and control force, making them ideal for high force applications such as testing heavy-duty materials like concrete and metals.
