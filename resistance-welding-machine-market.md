<p><p>Resistance welding machines are classified into three main types based on their level of automation: Manual, Semi-Automatic, and Automatic. Manual resistance welding machines require operators to manually load and operate the machine. Semi-Automatic machines involve some level of automation in the welding process, such as automated electrode movement. Automatic resistance welding machines are fully automated, requiring minimal human intervention in the welding process. Each type of machine caters to different production volumes and levels of operator skill.</p></p>
