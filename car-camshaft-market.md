<p><p>The car camshaft market consists of two main types: cast camshafts and assembled camshafts. Cast camshafts are made by pouring molten metal into a mold, allowing for precise and complex shapes to be created. Assembled camshafts, on the other hand, are made by combining individual components such as lobes, journals, and tubes together to form the final product. Both types have their own advantages and disadvantages in terms of cost, production process, and performance.</p></p>
