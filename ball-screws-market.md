<p><ul><li>Internal Circulation Ballscrew</li><li>External Circulation Ballscrew</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ball screws are mechanical devices used for converting rotary motion into linear motion. There are two main types of ball screws on the market: internal circulation ball screws and external circulation ball screws. Internal circulation ball screws have the balls recirculating within the nut to reduce friction and improve efficiency. External circulation ball screws have the balls recirculating outside the nut, which allows for longer stroke lengths and higher load capacities. Both types offer high precision and are commonly used in various industrial applications.</p></p>
