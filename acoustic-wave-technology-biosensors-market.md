<p><p>Acoustic wave technology biosensors utilize quartz crystal microbalance (QCM) sensors and surface acoustic wave (SAW) sensors to detect analytes in various applications. QCM sensors measure changes in frequency caused by mass binding to the surface of a quartz crystal, while SAW sensors detect changes in surface acoustic waves due to interactions with target molecules. Both technologies offer high sensitivity and real-time monitoring capabilities, making them valuable tools in biomedical, environmental, and food safety industries.</p></p>
