<p><p>Desktop UV curing systems are available in two main types - Spot-Type and Line-Type. Spot-Type systems emit concentrated UV light in a specific area, making them suitable for curing small parts or localized sections. On the other hand, Line-Type systems emit UV light in a continuous line, making them ideal for curing larger areas or continuous applications. Both types of systems offer precise control over the curing process and are widely used in industries such as electronics, 3D printing, and automotive for their efficiency and effectiveness.</p></p>
