On the other hand, Broadband PLC is capable of transmitting data at higher speeds, making it suitable for applications like video streaming and high-speed internet access. Both types of Powerline Carrier technology offer reliable communication over existing power lines, providing a cost-effective solution for various industries.</p></p>
