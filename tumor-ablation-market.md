<p><p>Tumor ablation market encompasses various technologies such as Radiofrequency (RF) Ablation, Microwave Ablation, Cryoablation, and Other Technologies. RF ablation uses heat generated by high-frequency electrical currents to destroy tumor cells. Microwave ablation utilizes electromagnetic waves to heat and destroy tumor tissue. Cryoablation freezes and kills tumor cells by inserting a cold probe into the tumor. Other technologies may include high-intensity focused ultrasound (HIFU) or laser ablation. These technologies offer minimally invasive alternatives to surgery for treating tumors.</p></p>
