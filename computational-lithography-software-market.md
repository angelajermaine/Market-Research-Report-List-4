<p><strong>The Computational Lithography Software Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>OPC</li><li>SMO</li><li>MPT</li><li>ILT</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Computational Lithography Software market includes three main types: Optical Proximity Correction (OPC), Source Mask Optimization (SMO), Multi-Patterning Technology (MPT), and Inverse Lithography Technology (ILT). OPC involves correcting images to account for optical distortions, SMO optimizes light source and mask designs, MPT uses multiple exposures to create complex patterns, and ILT calculates optimized mask patterns for high-resolution lithography. Each type caters to different needs in the semiconductor industry, from enhancing resolution to minimizing defects.</p></p>
