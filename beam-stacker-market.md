<p><p>Single Beam Storage and Double Beam Storage are two common market types for Beam Stackers. Single Beam Storage involves a single beam that supports the weight of the stored materials. This type is suitable for lighter loads and applications where space is limited. On the other hand, Double Beam Storage utilizes two beams to provide additional support and stability for heavier loads. This type is more commonly used in industrial settings where larger and heavier items need to be stored. Both types offer efficient and organized storage solutions for various industries.</p></p>
