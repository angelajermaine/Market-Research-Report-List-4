<p><strong>The Universal Testing Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electromechanical UTM</li><li>Hydraulic UTM</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Universal Testing Machines (UTM) are commonly categorized into two types: Electromechanical UTM and Hydraulic UTM. Electromechanical UTMs use an electric motor to apply force to the sample being tested, while Hydraulic UTMs use fluid power for the same purpose. Electromechanical UTMs are generally preferred for their precision and accuracy in testing materials with low to medium force requirements, while Hydraulic UTMs are preferred for testing high force applications due to their ability to generate higher forces efficiently. Both types are widely used in various industries for material testing purposes.</p></p>
