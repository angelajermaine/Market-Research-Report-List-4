Mechanical vulcanizers utilize mechanical force to apply pressure and heat to the tires for vulcanization, while hydraulic vulcanizers use hydraulic pressure to accomplish the same task. Both types of vulcanizers are used in the tire industry to ensure proper vulcanization and improve the overall quality and performance of tires. Customers can choose the type of vulcanizer based on their specific needs and production requirements.</p></p>
