<p><p>Ambient lighting refers to a type of lighting that provides overall illumination to a space. The market for ambient lighting includes various types such as recessed lights, surface mounted lights, suspended lights, track lights, and strip lights. Recessed lights are installed into the ceiling, surface mounted lights are attached directly to the ceiling, suspended lights hang from the ceiling, track lights are adjustable and mounted on a track, and strip lights are long, flexible strips that can be attached to various surfaces. Each type caters to different lighting needs and aesthetics.</p></p>
