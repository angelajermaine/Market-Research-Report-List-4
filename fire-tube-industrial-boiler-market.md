<p><p>A fire tube industrial boiler is a type of boiler in which hot gases from a fire pass through one or more tubes running through a sealed container of water. The heat of the gases is transferred through the walls of the tubes by thermal conduction, heating the water and ultimately creating steam. These boilers are commonly used in industrial settings for generating steam for various processes.</p><p>The Fire Tube Industrial Boiler Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 5.80% during the forecast period.
