Hot runner temperature controllers are used to maintain precise temperature levels in the hot runner system to ensure consistent and high-quality moldings. On the other hand, hot runner sequence timer controllers are responsible for controlling the sequence and timing of operations in the hot runner system, allowing for efficient and optimized production processes. Both types of controllers play a crucial role in the successful operation of hot runner systems in the manufacturing industry.</p></p>
