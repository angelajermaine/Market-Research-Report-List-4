<p><strong>The Artificial Tendons Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Vitro Tissue</li><li>Internal Tissue</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Artificial tendons can be classified into two main types - in vitro tissue and internal tissue. In vitro tissue artificial tendons are created outside of the body using bioengineered materials, such as collagen and synthetic polymers. These tendons are then implanted into the body to replace damaged or injured natural tendons. On the other hand, internal tissue artificial tendons are made from materials that can be implanted directly into the body, such as metal alloys or silicone. Both types of artificial tendons aim to restore function and mobility to patients with tendon injuries.</p></p>
