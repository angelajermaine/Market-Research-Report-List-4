<p><p>PCI Stents Market Types include Bare-metal Stents (BMS), Drug-eluting Stents (DES), and Bioresorbable Vascular Scaffold (BVS). Bare-metal stents are uncoated metal stents used to keep arteries open. Drug-eluting stents are coated with medication to prevent restenosis. Bioresorbable Vascular Scaffolds are temporary stents that dissolve over time, allowing the artery to return to its natural state. Each type serves a specific purpose in the treatment of coronary artery disease, providing options for patients and doctors to consider.</p></p>
