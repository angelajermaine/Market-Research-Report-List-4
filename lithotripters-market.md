There are three main types of lithotripters on the market: hydroelectric shock wave lithotripters, electromagnetic shock wave lithotripters, and others. Hydroelectric lithotripters use water-driven shock waves to break up stones, while electromagnetic lithotripters use electromagnetically-generated shock waves. The "others" category may include newer technologies or variations on existing methods of stone fragmentation, offering a range of options for healthcare providers and patients.</p></p>
