<p><p>Linear motion bearings can be categorized into two main types: rolling bearings and plain bearings. Rolling bearings are designed with rolling elements such as balls or rollers that reduce friction and allow for smooth motion along a linear axis. Plain bearings, on the other hand, do not have rolling elements and rely on a sliding surface for motion. Each type has its own advantages and is used in various applications depending on factors such as load capacity, speed, and maintenance requirements.</p></p>
