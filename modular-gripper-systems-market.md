<p><p>Modular Gripper Systems market includes various types such as parallel gripper systems and angular gripper systems. Parallel gripper systems are designed with two parallel jaws that move towards each other to grasp an object, providing a strong and stable grip. On the other hand, angular gripper systems have jaws that move at an angle to each other, allowing for a more versatile gripping capability. Both types offer different advantages based on the specific application requirements, making them popular choices in industries such as manufacturing and automation.</p></p>
