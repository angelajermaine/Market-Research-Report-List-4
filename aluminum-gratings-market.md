<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Aluminum Gratings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Press-locked Gratings</li><li>Swage-locked Gratings</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Aluminum gratings market includes two main types: press-locked gratings and swage-locked gratings. Press-locked gratings are manufactured by permanently locking cross bars into bearing bars through tremendous hydraulic pressure, providing strong and durable structure. Swage-locked gratings, on the other hand, are assembled by the cross bars being inserted into pre-punched holes in bearing bars and then swaged together, offering high strength and stability. Both types of gratings are widely used in various industries due to their superior quality and performance.</p></p>
