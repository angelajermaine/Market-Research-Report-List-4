<p><p>Outdoor humidity sensors are devices used to measure the amount of moisture in the air. There are two main types of outdoor humidity sensors: wired and wireless. Wired outdoor humidity sensors require a physical connection to a monitoring system, while wireless sensors transmit data wirelessly. Wired sensors are typically more reliable and accurate, but can be more difficult to install. Wireless sensors offer more flexibility in terms of placement and are easier to install, but may be less reliable in certain conditions. Both types of sensors are essential for monitoring and controlling humidity levels in outdoor environments.</p></p>
