<p><ul><li>Continuous Dehydration Equipment</li><li>Intermittent Dehydration Equipment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Continuous dehydration equipment is designed to operate continuously without the need for frequent stops or interruptions. It is often used for large-scale dehydration processes where a consistent output is necessary. On the other hand, intermittent dehydration equipment operates in cycles, with periods of dehydration followed by periods of rest. This type of equipment is suitable for smaller-scale operations or when a variable output is acceptable. Both types of equipment play a crucial role in the vegetable dehydration market, catering to different needs and preferences of manufacturers.</p></p>
