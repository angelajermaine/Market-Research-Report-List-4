<p><p>Thermoplastic Polyolefin (TPO) is a type of polymer blend made from a combination of polypropylene and ethylene. TPO is widely used in various industries due to its excellent weather resistance, durability, and cost-effectiveness. The market types of TPO include Polyethylene, Polypropylene, and Functional Polyolefins. Polyethylene TPO provides flexibility and impact resistance, while Polypropylene TPO offers high strength and stiffness. Functional Polyolefins are customized TPO blends with additional properties such as flame retardancy or UV resistance for specific applications.</p></p>
