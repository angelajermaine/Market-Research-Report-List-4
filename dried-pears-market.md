<p><p>Dried pears come in various market types, including Baked Dried and Freeze Dried. Baked Dried pears are made by baking fresh pears at a low temperature until they become dehydrated, resulting in a chewy texture and concentrated flavor. Freeze Dried pears are created by freezing fresh pears and then removing the moisture through a process called sublimation, which preserves the fruit's natural color, flavor, and nutrients. Both types of dried pears offer a convenient and delicious snack option for consumers.</p></p>
