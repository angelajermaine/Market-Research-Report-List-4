<p><ul><li>Annunciators</li><li>Notification Devices</li><li>Manual Pull Stations</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fire Alarm Equipment (FAS) market is segmented into three main types: annunciators, notification devices, and manual pull stations. Annunciators are used to display information about the status of the fire alarm system. Notification devices include horns, strobes, and speakers that alert building occupants of a fire. Manual pull stations are devices installed on walls to manually trigger the fire alarm system in case of an emergency. These three types of equipment play a crucial role in ensuring the safety of buildings and their occupants in the event of a fire.</p></p>
