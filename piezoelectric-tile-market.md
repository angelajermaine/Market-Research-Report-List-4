<p><p>Piezoelectric tile market is divided into two main types based on the material used: hard ceramics and soft ceramics. Hard ceramics, such as lead zirconate titanate (PZT) ceramics, are known for their high piezoelectric coefficient and are commonly used in industrial applications requiring high sensitivity and durability. Soft ceramics, such as polymer-based ceramics, are more flexible and lightweight, making them suitable for applications where flexibility and ease of installation are important. Both types of ceramics play a crucial role in the development of piezoelectric tile technology.</p></p>
