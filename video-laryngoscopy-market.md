<p><p>Video laryngoscopy market can be divided into two types - channelled video laryngoscopy and unchannelled video laryngoscopy. Channelled video laryngoscopy involves the use of a guiding channel to help insert the endotracheal tube into the patient's airway, making the process easier and more accurate. On the other hand, unchannelled video laryngoscopy does not use a guiding channel, requiring more skill and precision from the healthcare provider. Both types have their own advantages and are used in different clinical settings based on the needs of the patient.</p></p>
