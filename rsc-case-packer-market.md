<p><p>RSC Case Packer market is divided into two main types: Semi-automatic Packer and Fully Automatic Packer. Semi-automatic packers require some manual labor for loading products into cases before sealing, making them suitable for smaller scale production. On the other hand, Fully Automatic Packagers are capable of automatically picking, placing, and sealing cases without the need for human intervention, making them ideal for high-volume production. Both types cater to different market needs based on production scale and level of automation required.</p></p>
