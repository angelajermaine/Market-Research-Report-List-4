<p><p>High end milk powder is segmented into two main types: whole milk powder and skim milk powder. Whole milk powder is made from evaporating milk until only the solids remain, giving it a rich and creamy texture. On the other hand, skim milk powder is produced by removing the fat content from milk, resulting in a lower calorie and fat option. Both types of milk powder are popular in the high-end market due to their convenience, longer shelf life, and versatility in various recipes.</p></p>
