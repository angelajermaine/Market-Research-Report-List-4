<p><p>Dual-Channel Dissolved Oxygen Transmitters are available in different market types including 2-Wire, 3-Wire, and 4-Wire configurations. 2-Wire transmitters require only two wires for both power supply and signal output, making installation simpler and more cost-effective. 3-Wire transmitters have an additional wire for ground connection, offering improved stability and performance. 4-Wire transmitters separate the power supply and signal output into two separate pairs of wires, providing the highest level of accuracy and reliability. Each market type offers unique advantages to meet the varying needs of different applications.</p></p>
