Cloud-based ETL software is hosted on remote servers and accessible via the internet, offering scalability and flexibility for users. On the other hand, on-premises ETL software is installed and operated on a company's own servers, providing greater control and security over data. Both types have their own advantages and drawbacks, with cloud-based solutions being more cost-effective and on-premises solutions offering more customization options.</p></p>
