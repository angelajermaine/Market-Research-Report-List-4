<p><strong>The Pumped Hydroelectric Storage Turbines Market Industry Research by Application is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Open Loop</li><li>Closed Loop</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Pumped hydroelectric storage turbines are used in both open loop and closed loop systems for energy storage and generation. In open loop systems, water is sourced from natural water bodies and stored at a higher elevation during periods of low electricity demand. In closed loop systems, water is circulated between two reservoirs creating a closed system. Both systems allow for the generation of electricity during peak demand by releasing the stored water to drive turbines and produce power efficiently.</p></p>
