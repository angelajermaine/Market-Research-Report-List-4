<p><p>VOC gas sensor devices include PID (Photoionization Detector) and Metal-oxide Semiconductor types in the market. PID sensors are sensitive to a wide range of volatile organic compounds and provide real-time detection with high accuracy. Metal-oxide Semiconductor sensors operate by detecting changes in conductivity when exposed to VOCs. They are cost-effective and commonly used in indoor air quality monitoring applications. Both types of sensors play a crucial role in ensuring safety and environmental protection by monitoring and detecting harmful VOCs in various settings.</p></p>
