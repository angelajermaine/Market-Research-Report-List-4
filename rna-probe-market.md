<p><p>RNA probes are commonly used in molecular biology for the detection and analysis of specific RNA sequences. There are three main types of RNA probes: cRNA probe, single-stranded cDNA probe, and oligonucleotide probe. cRNA probes are synthesized complementary RNA sequences for target RNA detection, single-stranded cDNA probes are complementary DNA sequences for RNA detection, and oligonucleotide probes are short synthetic DNA or RNA sequences used for rapid and specific target detection. Each type of probe offers unique advantages for different applications in research and diagnostics.</p></p>
