<p><p>Flexible rubber sheeting market types include NBR Based, EPDM Based, Chloroprene Based, and Others. NBR Based rubber sheets are made from nitrile rubber and are known for their oil and fuel resistance. EPDM Based sheets are made from ethylene propylene diene monomer and are resistant to weathering and UV exposure. Chloroprene Based sheets are made from neoprene rubber and are resistant to chemicals and abrasion. Lastly, the Others category includes various types of rubber sheets with unique properties and applications.</p></p>
