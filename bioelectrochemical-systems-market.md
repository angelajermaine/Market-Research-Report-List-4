<p><p>Bioelectrochemical systems market primarily consists of two types: Microbial Fuel Cells (MFCs) and Microbial Electrolysis Cells (MECs). MFCs generate electricity through the metabolic activity of microorganisms, while MECs produce hydrogen or other chemicals by using electrical energy to drive microbial reactions. Both technologies have applications in wastewater treatment, energy production, and biofuel generation. The market for these systems is expected to grow as the demand for clean energy solutions increases worldwide.</p></p>
