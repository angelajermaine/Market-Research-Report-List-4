<p><p>Underwater drones are classified into two main types: Autonomous Underwater Vehicles (AUVs) and Remotely Operated Vehicles (ROVs). AUVs are self-propelled drones that can operate underwater without human intervention, collecting data and performing tasks independently. On the other hand, ROVs are controlled by a human operator from the surface and are used for tasks that require real-time control and manipulation of objects underwater. Both AUVs and ROVs play important roles in various industries such as oil and gas, marine research, and underwater exploration.</p></p>
