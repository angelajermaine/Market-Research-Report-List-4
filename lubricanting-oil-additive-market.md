<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Lubricanting Oil Additive Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Viscosity Index Improvers</li><li>Dispersants</li><li>Detergents</li><li>Inhibitor/Antiwear Agents</li><li>Antioxidants</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Lubricanting oil additives are categorized into different types based on their functions in enhancing the performance of lubricants. Viscosity index improvers improve the stability of lubricants at different temperatures. Dispersants prevent sludge and varnish build-up in the engine. Detergents help clean the engine by removing deposits. Inhibitor/Antiwear agents minimize friction and wear between moving parts. Antioxidants protect the lubricant from oxidation. There are also other additives in the market that serve various purposes such as foam inhibitors, pour point depressants, and rust inhibitors.</p></p>
