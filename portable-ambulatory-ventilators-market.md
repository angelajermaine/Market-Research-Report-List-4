<p><p>Portable ambulatory ventilators are devices used to provide respiratory support to patients who are mobile. There are three main types of ventilators in the market: electronic, pneumatic, and electro-pneumatic. Electronic ventilators utilize electrical signals to control airflow, while pneumatic ventilators use compressed gas to generate airflow. Electro-pneumatic ventilators combine both electrical and pneumatic systems for precise control of ventilation. Each type of ventilator offers unique features and benefits to meet the diverse needs of patients requiring respiratory support on the go.</p></p>
