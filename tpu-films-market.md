<p><p>TPU films are categorized into Polyester TPU Films and Polyether TPU Films based on the type of raw material used in their production. Polyester TPU Films offer high tensile strength and puncture resistance, making them suitable for applications requiring durability. Polyether TPU Films, on the other hand, provide excellent flexibility and resistance to chemicals, making them ideal for applications where flexibility is crucial. Apart from these two main types, there are other niche markets for TPU films, such as medical-grade films or films with special properties tailored to specific applications.</p></p>
