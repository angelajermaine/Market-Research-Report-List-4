<p><strong>The Industrial Machine Vision Lenses Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>C-Mount</li><li>CS-Mount</li><li>F-Mount</li><li>S-Mount</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Industrial machine vision lenses market can be classified into different types based on their mount types. C-Mount lenses are the most common and versatile, suitable for a wide range of applications. CS-Mount lenses are similar to C-Mount but with a shorter flange distance, ideal for compact camera setups. F-Mount lenses are designed for high-resolution imaging. S-Mount lenses are used for small cameras and have a shorter flange distance. Other mount types include T-Mount, M42-Mount, and others, each serving specific requirements in industrial machine vision applications.</p></p>
