<p><p>Refrigerant Gas Sensor Module Market includes two main types of sensors: NDIR (Non-Dispersive Infrared) Sensors and Metal Oxide Semiconductor Sensors. NDIR Sensors detect gases by analyzing how they absorb infrared light, making them highly accurate and reliable for detecting refrigerant gases. On the other hand, Metal Oxide Semiconductor Sensors operate by measuring changes in electrical conductivity when exposed to gases, making them versatile and cost-effective options for monitoring refrigerant gas levels in various applications.</p></p>
