<p><ul><li>Wired (Power Line Communication)</li><li>Wireless</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Connected street lights can be categorized into two types: wired, utilizing Power Line Communication (PLC), and wireless. Wired systems use existing power lines to communicate data between street lights and a central control system. This makes installation relatively easy and cost-effective. On the other hand, wireless systems rely on dedicated communication networks or protocols, like Zigbee or LoRa, to connect street lights. While wireless systems provide more flexibility and scalability, they may require additional infrastructure investment compared to wired systems.</p></p>
