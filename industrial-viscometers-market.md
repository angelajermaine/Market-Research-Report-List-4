<p><p>Industrial viscometers are used to measure the viscosity of fluids in various industries. In-line process viscometers are installed directly into the production line to continuously monitor viscosity during manufacturing processes. Portable viscometers are handheld devices that can be easily transported and used for viscosity measurements at different locations. Laboratory viscometers are stationary instruments typically used in research and development settings to accurately measure viscosity in controlled laboratory environments. Each type of viscometer serves a specific purpose in assessing viscosity in different industrial settings.</p></p>
