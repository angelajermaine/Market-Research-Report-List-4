<p><ul><li>Asphalt-Impregnated Paper or Fiberglass</li><li>Micro-Perforated</li><li>Spunbonded Nonwoven</li><li>Woven</li><li>Drainable House Wraps</li></ul></p>
<p><p>House wraps are breathable membranes used as a weather-resistant barrier to protect buildings from moisture infiltration. Asphalt-Impregnated Paper or Fiberglass wraps are traditional and relatively inexpensive. Micro-Perforated wraps allow vapor to escape while keeping out liquid water. Spunbonded Nonwoven wraps are lightweight and tear-resistant. Woven wraps are durable and provide excellent protection. Drainable House Wraps offer added protection by allowing any trapped moisture to drain away. Each type of house wrap has specific characteristics and benefits to suit different construction needs.</p></p>
