<p><p>Electronystagmography testing market encompasses various types of tests used to evaluate disorders related to the vestibular (balance) system. These tests include Videonystagmography (VNG), which records eye movements to assess balance function; Rotary Chair Test, which measures how well the balance organs in the inner ear are working; Computerized Dynamic Visual Acuity (DVA) and Computerized Dynamic Posturography (CDP), which evaluate visual and postural stability; Subjective Visual Vertical, which assesses perception of vertical orientation; and Vestibular Evoked Myogenic Potential (VEMP) testing, which measures muscle responses to sound stimuli to assess vestibular function.</p></p>
