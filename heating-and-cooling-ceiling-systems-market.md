<p><p>Heating and cooling ceiling systems are divided into two main types: water heating and electric heating. Water heating systems utilize a network of pipes to circulate hot water through the ceiling panels to provide warmth, while electric heating systems use electric coils to generate heat. Water heating systems are more energy-efficient and cost-effective in the long run, while electric heating systems are easier to install and maintain. Both types offer efficient heating and cooling solutions for various spaces.</p></p>
