<p><p>Rotary evaporators are classified into two main types: horizontal and vertical. Horizontal rotary evaporators are used in industries where space is limited, as they have a compact design and small footprint. They are ideal for small-scale operations and research labs. On the other hand, vertical rotary evaporators are commonly used in larger industrial settings where higher volumes of samples need to be processed. They offer higher capacity and efficiency compared to horizontal models. Both types play a crucial role in distillation and separation processes in various industries.</p></p>
