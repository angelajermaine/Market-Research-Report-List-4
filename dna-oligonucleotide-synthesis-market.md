<p><p>DNA Oligonucleotide Synthesis Market includes various types of oligonucleotides such as Primers, Probes, Intermediate-scale Synthesis Oligos, Large-scale Synthesis Oligos, Linkers, and Adaptors. Primers are short nucleotide sequences used to initiate DNA replication. Probes are used to detect complementary DNA sequences. Intermediate-scale Synthesis Oligos are synthesized in moderate quantities for research purposes. Large-scale Synthesis Oligos are produced in bulk for commercial applications. Linkers and Adaptors are used to join different DNA fragments together. These different types cater to various research and commercial needs in the biotechnology industry.</p></p>
