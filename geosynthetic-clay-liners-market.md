<p><p>Geosynthetic Clay Liners are used for environmental containment applications, such as landfill liners and caps. Nonwoven Geotextile liners are made of synthetic fibers bonded together, providing filtration and drainage capabilities. Woven Geotextile liners are made of tightly woven synthetic fibers, offering strength and stability. Natural Sodium Bentonite liners consist of a layer of bentonite clay sandwiched between geotextile fabrics, swelling when hydrated to create a impermeable barrier. Each type of liner has specific advantages depending on the project requirements.</p></p>
