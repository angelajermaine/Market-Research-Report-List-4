<p><p>Industrial high-shear mixers are classified into three main types: batch high shear mixers, inline high shear mixers, and others. Batch high shear mixers are used for mixing materials in a contained vessel, allowing for precise control over the mixing process. Inline high shear mixers are used for continuous mixing applications where materials are mixed as they flow through a pipeline. Other types of high shear mixers include ultra-high shear mixers and bottom-entry mixers, which are used for specific mixing applications that require different mixing techniques.</p></p>
