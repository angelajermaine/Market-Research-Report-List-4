<p><p>NTC thermistor sensors are available in various market types to suit different applications. Radial type sensors have leads extending from the ceramic body. Diode type sensors have a diode chip inside the ceramic body. Wire bonding type sensors use wire bonding technology for connections. Film type sensors have a thin film of semiconducting material on the ceramic body. SMD type sensors are surface-mount devices for easy installation. Wire type sensors have wires attached directly to the ceramic body. Other market types include customized designs catering to specific requirements.</p></p>
