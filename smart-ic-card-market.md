<p><p>Smart IC cards come in three main types: Contactless IC cards, which use radio frequency technology for communication and do not require physical contact with a reader; Contact IC cards, which need to be physically inserted into a card reader for data transmission; and Dual Interface IC cards, which combine both contactless and contact capabilities in a single card. These different types cater to various applications and industries, offering convenience, security, and versatility in different usage scenarios.</p></p>
