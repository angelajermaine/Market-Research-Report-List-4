<p><ul><li>Pressure Spray Dryer</li><li>Stream Spray Dryer</li><li>Centrifuge Spray Dryer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The spray dryer market can be classified into three main types based on the method of atomization: Pressure Spray Dryer, Stream Spray Dryer, and Centrifuge Spray Dryer. Pressure spray dryers use high pressure nozzles to atomize the liquid feed, while stream spray dryers use steam to atomize the feed. Centrifuge spray dryers utilize a rotating disk or wheel to atomize the feed. Each type offers unique advantages and is suitable for different applications within industries such as food, pharmaceuticals, and chemicals.</p></p>
