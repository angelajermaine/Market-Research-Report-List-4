<p><p>Genotyping market involves various technologies to analyze genetic variations in individuals. Polymerase Chain Reaction (PCR) is a widely used method for amplifying DNA segments. Sequencing determines the exact sequence of nucleotides in DNA. Microarray technology allows for the simultaneous analysis of multiple genetic markers. Electrophoresis separates DNA fragments based on size. MALDI-TOF (Matrix-Assisted Laser Desorption/Ionization Time of Flight) is a mass spectrometry technique used for genotyping. Each of these technologies offers unique advantages for studying genetic variations and understanding their impact on health and disease.</p></p>
