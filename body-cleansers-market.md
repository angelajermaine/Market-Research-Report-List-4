<p><ul><li>Acidic Body Cleanser</li><li>Alkalic Body Cleanser</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Body cleansers can be categorized into two types based on their pH levels: acidic body cleansers and alkalic body cleansers. Acidic body cleansers have a lower pH level, which helps maintain the skin's natural acidity and prevent bacterial growth. These cleansers are gentle and suitable for sensitive skin. Alkalic body cleansers, on the other hand, have a higher pH level, which can effectively remove dirt and oil from the skin. However, they can be harsh and drying, making them more suitable for oily or acne-prone skin.</p></p>
