<p><p>Shaftless elevators, also known as machine room-less elevators, are a type of elevator that eliminates the need for a traditional shaft. Instead, these elevators use a compact gearless traction machine that is installed directly in the hoistway or on top of the elevator cab. This design saves space and construction costs, making shaftless elevators a popular choice for modern buildings with limited space.</p><p>The shaftless elevators market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 9.8% during the forecast period.
