<p><p>The fingerprint recognition chip market is divided into three main types: capacitive, ultrasonic, and optical fingerprint recognition. Capacitive fingerprint recognition relies on measuring the electrical charge differences in the skin's ridges and valleys. Ultrasonic fingerprint recognition uses sound waves to create a 3D image of the fingerprint. Optical fingerprint recognition captures an image of the fingerprint using light and analyzes the unique patterns. Each type has its own benefits and limitations, catering to various needs in security and biometric applications.</p></p>
