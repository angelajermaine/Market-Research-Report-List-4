<p><p>Thermally conductive additives are used in various industries to enhance thermal conductivity in materials. Thermoplastic conductive additives are additives that can be easily molded when heated, making them ideal for applications where flexibility is essential. On the other hand, thermosetting conductive additives are additives that undergo a chemical reaction when heated, leading to a permanent solid state, making them ideal for applications requiring high heat resistance and structural stability. Both types of additives play a crucial role in improving the thermal properties of materials in different ways.</p></p>
